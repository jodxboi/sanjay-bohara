About Us

At Advocate Sampatlal & Sanjay Bohara, we provide comprehensive legal services tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. Our Services

Our legal practice focuses on civil, revenue, and property disputes, handling a wide array of cases with skill and professionalism. With over 55 years in the field, we bring an unparalleled level of experience and a dedication to excellence in every case we undertake.

Our core services include:

  • Legal Representation: Our team represents clients in civil, revenue, and property cases, ensuring their rights are protected and their voices heard in every legal setting.
  • Landlord and Tenant Litigation: We assist clients in resolving disputes between landlords and tenants, providing expertise in eviction cases, lease disagreements, and other rental issues.
  • Legal Consulting: We offer expert guidance for all legal matters, empowering our clients with knowledge to make informed decisions regarding their cases.
  • Property Dispute Litigation: We have a deep understanding of property law and are dedicated to resolving property conflicts, including inheritance disputes and land ownership cases.

Our experience in the field is strengthened by our loyalty to clients and commitment to upholding the law. We work closely with each client, understanding their needs and crafting a legal strategy that aligns with their goals. Our firm is known for its integrity, and we are proud of the relationships we have built over decades of service in Udaipur.
